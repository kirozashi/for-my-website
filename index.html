The Next Phase: where the end meets the beginning

Finishing Senior High School is such an achievement, it feels like finishing an endless marathon. It is where you can put down all the weights you've been carrying through the journey. But I've realized, it's not really the end—it's just a pause, a pause that let us plan and prepare ourselves for a new adventure. And now, I'll tell you my plan, a plan that dictate my life in the future. Right after graduation, I'll talk to my parents about the course and school that I'll take in college. I'll look for scholarships and prepare myself for examinations and admission test for schools. i will also start to build my discipline and consistency, because i need to be independent.

—Eliseo R. Mendoza
